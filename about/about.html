Our anti-sleeping glasses are designed to keep drivers safe on the road by preventing drowsy driving accidents. Equipped with special sensors, these glasses can detect when a driver is feeling sleepy. Once sleepiness is detected, the glasses activate an alert system, which includes a loud buzzer to wake up the driver and a message sent to the vehicle's onboard computer to slow down. If the driver doesn't wake up after several alerts, the glasses instruct the vehicle to stop completely, preventing potential accidents. Additionally, the glasses turn on special parking lights to alert other drivers of the stopped vehicle. Simultaneously, the glasses send a message to the manager of the truck or bus, providing the live location of the vehicle and alerting them to the driver's condition, enabling timely intervention and assistance. This integrated approach ensures that drivers remain alert and safe while on the road, while also notifying relevant parties in case of emergencies.
